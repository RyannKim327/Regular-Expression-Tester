For the regex, credits to this repossitory for the Regular Expressions Snippets also in Rovie Francisco. Credits also to Jerson Carin for others regex related topics.

  • . - Any character except new line
  • \w - World or alpha numeric characters including underscore
  • \s - Whitespaces
  • \d - Digit or numerical
  • \W - Not an alphanumeric character
  • \S - Not a whitespace
  • \b - Word Boundary
  • \B - Not a Word Boundary
  • ^ - Beginning of a String
  • $ - End of a String
  • [] - Matches characters in the bracket
  • [^] - Matches characters NOT in the bracket
  • | - Either or OR
  • () - Group
  • * - 0 or more
  • + - 1 or more
  • ? - 0 or one
  • {n} - Exact number example: {3}
  • {from, to} - Range of Numbers example: {3, 4}
